Independently Verified Fuel Savings

Computer Modeling

Initial validation of the reduction in drag and fuel savings was done using computational fluid dynamics (CFD), and showed that the opportunity at the gap merited further consideration.

Wind tunnel

At the Automotive Research Center (ARC) facility, the only SmartWay certified wind tunnel, modeling suggested up to 4% fuel savings on sleeper cabs.

Track testing

On-track testing in Canada supervised by PIT Group demonstrated the device operating on a truck in a track setting

On-highway

Weighted fuel tank testing demonstrated real-world fuel savings on a customer truck on the interstate. North American Council for Freight Efficiency was 3rd party witness of testing.
